Crystal Collector Game!

Directions:


You'll see a number, your starting score, and 4 crystals below. When clicked on, each crystal will add a random amount to your score.

In order to win the game you need to get your score to equal the game score. However, if your score exceeds the game score, you lose!

After each round the number resets, and so does the value of each of the crystals!

Good Luck!
